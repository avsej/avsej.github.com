An article showing how to integrate [couchbase gem][1] into your
[EventMachine][2] application.

> To demonstrate the integration, lets build simple chat application
> using EventMachine and the add logging for all events there to
> Couchbase bucket. It is extremely easy to build asynchronous
> application with EventMachine and to prove it I will put the
> complete source here, in this post (also found in
> [examples/chat-em][3] directory of the gem sources).


[1]: https://rubygems.org/gems/couchbase
[2]: https://rubygems.org/gems/eventmachine
[3]: https://github.com/couchbase/couchbase-ruby-client/tree/master/examples/chat-em
